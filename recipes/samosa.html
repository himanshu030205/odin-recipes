How to make Samosa?

Description:

A samosa is a popular Indian snack characterized by its triangular pastry shell filled with a

savory mixture, often featuring ingredients like spiced potatoes, peas, and various seasonings.

These delicious parcels are typically deep-fried to a golden crisp, offering a delightful blend

of flavours and textures.

Ingredients:

  • Maida or White-flour
  • Onion
  • Peas
  • Spices
  • Green Chillies
  • Mustard or Soyabean oil

Steps to follow:

  1. Prepare Filling: Cook a spiced potato and pea mixture & let it cool.
  2. Assemble Samosa: Fill pastry dough with mixture,fold,and seal into triangle shapes
  3. Bake: Place on a baking sheet, brush with oil, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes until golden.
  4. Serve: Let them cool slightly and enjoy with your favorite dip.
