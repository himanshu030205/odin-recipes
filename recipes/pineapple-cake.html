How to bake a delicious Pineapple-Cake?

Description:

Indulge in the tropical delight of a moist pineapple cake infused with sweet, tangy

pineapple bits, and a luscious cream cheese frosting. A slice of paradise on your plate!

Savor the perfect balance of tropical sweetness and creamy decadence in every bite of

our irresistible pineapple cake.

Ingredients:

  • Flour and Sugar
  • Pineapple
  • Butter and oil
  • Eggs
  • Baking Soda and salt
  • Cream cheese
  • Vanilla (optional)

Steps for baking:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C), grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Cream butter, sugar, and oil. Add eggs, vanilla, and pineapple.
  3. Mix in dry ingredients gradually.
  4. Divide batter between pans, bake for 25-30 mins, and cool.
  5. For frosting, beat cream cheese, butter, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  6. Frost cooled cakes and enjoy!
