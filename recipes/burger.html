How to make a delicious burger?

Descriptions:

A veg burger is a delightful culinary creation featuring a plant-based patty,

typically made from ingredients like vegetables, legumes, or grains.t's often

served in a soft bun and topped with a variety of fresh vegetables, condiments,

and sauces, delivering a satisfying, flavorful experience.

Ingredients:

  • Bun
  • Patty like fried mashed potato
  • Vegetables - Onion,Lettuce,Tomato
  • Sauce or ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • Oil - very consistent amount

Steps to make a delicious burger:

  1. Cut the bun into two half from its mid portion
  2. Now apply the oil or butter over it and leave it over medium heat
  3. Cut your vegetables appropriately, to be fit inside your burger
  4. Assemble the ingredients one over the other and add some sauce and mayo
  5. Enjoy your burger!
