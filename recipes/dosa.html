How to make south-indian style dosa?

Descriptions:

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter.

It's a popular dish known for its savory flavor and versatility, often served with

accompaniments like coconut chutney and sambar. Whether enjoyed plain or filled with

various ingredients, dosa is a beloved breakfast or snack in India and beyond,appreciated

for its delightful taste and texture.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Urad dal (black gram) batter
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps to follow:

  1. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle until hot.
  2. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot surface.
  3. Quickly spread the batter into a thin, round shape.
  4. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden and crispy.
  5. Fold and serve with chutney and sambar.
